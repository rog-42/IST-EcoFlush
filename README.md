# 💧 EcoFlush — Web Prototype for a Water Tracker App

This project is a **functional prototype** of a water‑saving mobile app, created for the **Water Talks** course at my university.  
It also served as a personal exercise to learn more about **web development**, especially Vue 3, reactive UI patterns, and browser‑based persistence.

The prototype runs entirely in the browser using:

- **Vue 3 via CDN** (no build tools)
- **HTML + modular CSS**
- **JavaScript modules**
- **localStorage** for saving daily usage, XP, challenges, and history

> **Note:**  
> The user interface is in **Portuguese**, as required for the course presentation.

---

## 📱 Features Included in This Prototype

- **Track Daily Water Usage:**
  - Keep track of water consumed through different actions like **showers**, **flushes**, and **custom amounts** (like general water usage).
  - Automatic calculation of **liters consumed**.
  - **Daily** and **monthly totals** for your water consumption.

- **XP and Leveling System:**
  - **XP** is earned through water-saving actions (such as short showers, half flushes, etc.).
  - Leveling up as you gain more XP, showing your progress in the app.
  
- **Daily Challenges with XP Rewards:**
  - Participate in daily challenges related to water conservation.
  - Earn XP based on completing each challenge (e.g., taking short showers, flushing with lower water usage).
  - Challenges encourage more sustainable water habits.

- **Leaderboard:**
  - A **monthly leaderboard** showing top users based on the amount of water saved.
  - **Manual friends list**: Add and compete with your friends for the highest ranking.
  
- **Social & Community Engagement:**
  - Join and participate in **community competitions** with others:
    - Compete with your **school**, **local community** (e.g., EPAL), or even a **global cause** (such as UNICEF's water-related missions).
  - Track your progress within your community’s ranking.

- **Water Meter Reading ("WaterReal"):**
  - Receive notifications to take a picture of your **water meter**.
  - Track real water usage and improve accuracy with **photo submissions** of your water meter reading.
  - Track how efficiently you’re saving water compared to others.
  
- **Water Drop Mascot:**
  - A **mascot** accompanies the user through the app to make the experience fun and rewarding.
  - You can customize your mascot's appearance based on XP gained.

- **Mobile-First UI:**
  - The app’s layout is designed to be **mobile-first**, offering an intuitive and easy-to-navigate experience on smartphones.

- **Water Conservation Notifications:**
  - The app sends you reminders and notifications about your daily water usage and upcoming tasks, like submitting your water meter reading.
  
- **Rewards Store:**
  - **XP** earned in the app can be used to redeem rewards such as discounts, digital services, or even access to special features.

## 🌟 Planned Features (Full App Vision)

The prototype represents only a small part of the full EcoFlush concept. Below is the complete set of **planned features** for the full app experience.

---

### **1. Gamified Water‑Saving Missions**
- Users complete real‑world water‑saving actions (e.g., short showers, reusing cold water, checking for leaks).
- Each mission grants **XP** immediately, adding to the gamification of the water-saving journey.
- XP can be used to **customize the user’s Water Drop avatar**.
- The goal is to make water conservation **fun, rewarding, and habit‑forming**.

---

### **2. Meter Reading Challenges**
- Users receive notifications from **“WaterReal”** and have **24 hours** to submit a **photo of their water meter**.
- The app tracks real consumption, updates rankings, and gives XP rewards for successful submissions.
- The most efficient users receive **real-world rewards** through partner brands (e.g., discounts, giveaways).

---

### **3. Community & Social Competition**
- Users can compete with:
  - **Friends**
  - **Classmates**
  - **Neighbors**
- Participate in **weekly** and **monthly** rankings to add a competitive element.
- The app supports adding friends manually and shows them in a **scrollable leaderboard**.

---

### **4. Environmental Impact Initiatives**
- Users can join **collective water‑saving challenges** that contribute to **donations to NGOs**.
- Focus on supporting **sanitation infrastructure** in vulnerable regions.
- Saved cubic meters of water are translated into **donations** and contribute towards the global mission.

---

### **5. Rewards Store**
- **XP** can be exchanged for rewards, reinforcing positive behavior with real incentives.
- Example rewards might include:
  - Discounts on **cinema tickets**, **subscriptions**, etc.
  - Special features or status within the app to encourage more engagement.
  
---

### **6. News & Awareness Tab**
- A dedicated section with curated articles and content about:
  - Water **scarcity**
  - **Waste reduction**
  - **Environmental policy**
  - **Global water challenges**
- Helps users stay **informed** and **engaged** with the wider environmental movement.

---

### **7. Water Conservation Stats & Progress Tracking**
- **Water usage stats** are tracked, showing daily and monthly trends.
- XP progress and **user level** tracking encourage users to improve their water-saving behaviors.

---

### **8. Competitions with Schools and Local Communities**
- Users can join competitions within their **school** or **neighborhood** to compete on water-saving practices.
- **School competition** ranks users in a specific institution.
- **Local competitions** (e.g., EPAL competitions) track users within a residential area, with community-based rewards and rankings.

---

### **9. Social Features for Connecting with Others**
- Adding friends and participating in community efforts create a **social network** aspect to the app.
- Progress can be compared with **friends**, and users are encouraged to participate in **group challenges**.

---

### **10. Full Integration with Environmental Organizations**
- Users can join a **global water-saving cause** (e.g., UNICEF, global water challenges) and track their contributions toward a **larger environmental mission**.
- **Progress bars** show how much users have contributed to goals like **funding water infrastructure** in developing countries.

---
