# 💧 Water Tracker — Prototype for the *Water Talks* Course

This project is a **functional prototype** of a water‑saving mobile app, created for the **Water Talks** course at my university.  
It also served as a personal exercise to learn more about **web development**, especially Vue 3, reactive UI patterns, and browser‑based persistence.

The prototype runs entirely in the browser using:

- **Vue 3 via CDN** (no build tools)
- **HTML + modular CSS**
- **JavaScript modules**
- **localStorage** for saving daily usage, XP, challenges, and history

> **Note:**  
> The user interface is in **Portuguese**, as required for the course presentation.

---

## 📱 Features Included in This Prototype

- Track daily water usage (showers, flushes, custom amounts)  
- Automatic calculation of liters consumed  
- Daily and monthly totals  
- XP and leveling system  
- Daily challenges with XP rewards  
- Social leaderboard with manually added friends  
- Mobile‑first layout with simple navigation  

---

## 🌟 Planned Features (Full App Vision)

The prototype represents only a small part of the full EcoFlush concept.  
Below is the complete set of **planned features** for the full app experience.

---

### **1. Gamified Water‑Saving Missions**
- Users complete real‑world water‑saving actions (short showers, reusing cold water, checking for leaks, etc.)  
- Each mission grants **XP** immediately  
- XP can be used to **customize the user’s Water Drop avatar**  
- The goal is to make water conservation **fun, rewarding, and habit‑forming**

---

### **2. Meter Reading Challenges**
- Users receive notifications from “WaterReal”  
- They have **24 hours** to submit a **photo of their water meter**  
- The app tracks real consumption and updates rankings  
- The most efficient users receive **real‑world rewards** through partner brands  

---

### **3. Community & Social Competition**
- Compete with:
  - Friends  
  - Classmates  
  - Neighbors  
- Weekly and monthly rankings  
- Designed to encourage participation and friendly competition  

---

### **4. Environmental Impact Initiatives**
- Users can join **collective water‑saving challenges**  
- Saved cubic meters of water are converted into **donations to NGOs**  
- Focus on supporting sanitation infrastructure in vulnerable regions  
- Encourages users to see their savings as part of a **larger environmental mission**

---

### **5. Rewards Store**
- XP can be exchanged for perks  
- Example (imaginary) rewards:
  - Discounts on cinema tickets  
  - Free months of digital services  
- Designed to reinforce sustainable habits through positive incentives  

---

### **6. News & Awareness Tab**
- A dedicated section with curated articles about:
  - Water scarcity  
  - Waste reduction  
  - Environmental policy  
  - Global water challenges  
- Helps users stay informed and engaged with the topic

## ✨ Final Note

This is an **academic prototype**, not a full product.
